Facilities

Facilities at Come'N'Learn

Our campus mixes old-school chalkboards with maker tools: a timber workshop, robotics bay, greenhouse, and a library that stays open late during exam season. Outdoor classes happen on the courtyard steps when the weather smiles.

  • STEM & arts labs with shared project storage
  • Sound-treated rehearsal rooms for music and drama
  • Sports pitch and indoor court for year-round play

Top-Shop Menu

Today at the Top-Shop

The Top-Shop is our student-run canteen corner: affordable plates, bold flavors, and a weekly “top board” voted by learners. Vegetarian and halal-friendly options are always on rotation.

  • House flatbread — herb butter, roasted veg, yogurt drizzle
  • Scholar bowl — grains, chickpeas, citrus dressing
  • Top-Shop brownie — small batch, different topping each Friday

Mosque

The Come'N'Learn mosque

A calm, well-lit prayer space on campus with ablution facilities nearby. Jumu'ah is hosted when numbers allow, and visiting scholars sometimes lead short reflections after Maghrib during term.

Hostel

The Come'N'Learn hostel

Dorm life here is intentionally structured: study hours, house mentors, and weekend outings that are optional but rarely skipped. Rooms are shared in small clusters so nobody drifts alone.

  • Quiet floors during exam weeks
  • Shared kitchens for light snacks (with safety orientation)
  • Laundry and storage on each wing
