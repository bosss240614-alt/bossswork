Leadership

Dr. Amina Bello

Director, Come'N'Learn

Stock photo for the demo — replace with your director's official portrait.

I lead Come'N'Learn with a simple promise: every learner should feel known, challenged, and safe to try again. Our timetable blends strong academics with craft, faith-informed character, and boarding rhythms that teach responsibility without squeezing out joy.

Before joining Come'N'Learn, I taught and led in schools that mixed project work with traditional exams; I still visit classrooms weekly because policy should never drift too far from the bell.

Parents and students are welcome to bring questions about pathways, boarding, or faith provision — my door stays open after morning drop-off on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
